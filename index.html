Moon Undies
Goldie is a 23 yr old in the year 2100. Her country has outlawed planned parenthood clinics, due to an abortion opposition, and women's health services have declined. Thankfully, Goldie lives in a city where a "Pink Matter" clinic has opened, based on a movement of the same name happening in the feminist community to provide holistic health resources to women. There, Goldie purchashed a "Moon Undies" bundle.



Made with gentle ingredients from Mother Earth herself, Moon Undies help Goldie keep track of her cycle, and let her know of any imbalances. She can look at her information in the comfort of her own home, as we use augmented holographic technology to share stats on her bedroom mirror!
